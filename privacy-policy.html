Privacy Policy

How we handle your information and protect your privacy.

Last updated: May 2026

Health Insurance Info ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information from our visitors:

  • Email address: If you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address.
  • Usage data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics (pages visited, time spent) to improve our content.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only for:

  • Sending you updates and new guides (only if you subscribe)
  • Improving our website content and user experience
  • Responding to your inquiries if you contact us

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated data for analytics purposes.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies for basic website functionality. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external sites (IRDAI, government portals, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.

Note: This is an informational website. We do not collect personal data beyond what you voluntarily provide through the subscription form. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising or marketing purposes.
