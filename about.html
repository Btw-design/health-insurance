About Us

We help Indian consumers make informed health insurance decisions through clear, unbiased, and accessible information.

Our Purpose

Why We Exist

Health insurance in India can be complex. We simplify it.

Health Insurance Info is an independent, non-commercial resource dedicated to helping individuals and families in India understand health insurance. We believe that everyone deserves access to clear, accurate, and unbiased information about their health coverage options.

Our team researches and compiles information from official government sources, regulatory bodies, and industry-standard practices to present you with reliable educational content. We do not sell insurance, collect personal data, or provide personalized financial advice.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Accuracy

We verify information from official government and regulatory sources before publishing.

Independence

We are not affiliated with any insurance company. Our content is unbiased and objective.

Clarity

We explain complex insurance concepts in simple, everyday language that anyone can understand.
